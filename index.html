Sukh – A Legacy of Love, part of the Sukh Foundation, is a heartfelt tribute to a beloved father, Sukhwinder Singh Dhanoa, whose spirit of kindness lives on in every meal we serve. Help us feed and empower children in underserved communities across Kenya

Rooted in compassion and fueled by love, our mission is to feed young hearts and nourish bright futures — starting in Kenya and reaching wherever children need care. We believe that no child should go hungry, and that every act of giving, no matter how small, can transform a life.

With each plate of food, we honour a legacy built on love.

We are a registered community-based organisation in Kenya, founded in 2025, dedicated to uplifting lives through compassion-driven initiatives.

Our flagship program, the School Feeding and Education Support Initiative, addresses child hunger and promotes access to quality education across underserved communities.

We envision a society where every child is well-nourished, safe, and equipped to build a better future. Through our feeding program, educational support, and youth empowerment activities, we are building strong, resilient communities founded on dignity, inclusion, and sustainable development.

Our Mission

To nourish children in underserved communities with love and dignity. Through every meal and act of kindness, we uplift lives, strengthen communities, and remind every child they are seen, supported, and never alone.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every child begins their day with a full stomach, a hopeful heart, and the opportunity to thrive — regardless of where they are born or the challenges they face.

Initiatives

Initiatives that power our mission

Feeding the Future

We provide daily, nutritious meals to children in underserved schools across Kenya. A full stomach fuels concentration, performance, and hope.

Education Support

From school supplies to tuition aid, we support children’s access to quality education and empower them to pursue their dreams.

Community & Youth Empowerment

Workshops, mentorship, and community activities equip young people with life skills, confidence, and purpose.

What We Believe

We believe every child deserves a fair chance — to learn, grow, and lead a dignified life. Kindness, equity, and community are the foundation of lasting change, and we carry these values in everything we do.

Where We Work

We operate in rural and urban communities across Kenya — including Nairobi, Kisumu, Turkana, and parts of the Coast — where needs are urgent and opportunities limited. Our reach continues to grow with every partnership and volunteer effort.

Projects

Recent Projects We have Worked On

Bringing Teachers Together to Nurture Brighter Futures

We host heartfelt seminars with local teachers, sharing simple yet powerful nutrition tips that help children grow stronger, healthier, and more confident—both in the classroom and beyond.

Visiting Schools to See Smiles, Not Empty Stomachs

We regularly visit our partner schools to connect with the children, listen to their stories, and ensure that every meal served is making a real difference in their growth and learning.

Walking Into Homes, Listening With Love

We sit with families in their homes, not just to check on meals, but to truly understand their challenges and share ideas for affordable, nutritious eating that brings dignity and hope to the dinner table.

Our Mission Impact So Far

The Sukh Legacy Foundation has been able to reach many children, women and people living in underprivileged communities in Kenya. We are committed to making a difference in the lives of those we serve, and we are grateful for the support of our partners and donors who make this work possible.
